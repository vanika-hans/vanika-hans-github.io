ELP Mentors

Over the course of my ELP career, I've been fortunate enough to get to know Sai Radhakrishnan. After pursuing a degree in Chemical and Biological Engineering at CU Boulder, Sai went on to pursue Med School. She is currently in her third year of Med School in Houston, Texas. She is originally from Colorado. Talking with Sai has been an extremely enlightening experience for me, as she is truly full of so many stories and experiences to share. Her passion for mentoring and self-exploration is truly inspring, and it's truly been wonderful being her Mentee.
